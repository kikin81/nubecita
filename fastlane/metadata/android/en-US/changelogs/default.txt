Say hello to nubecita — a fast, native Bluesky client for Android, built by an independent developer.
Dive into a smoother social experience. In our v1 launch, you can:
• Scroll a silky timeline with autoplaying video and photo carousels.
• Open threads, like, reply, and repost seamlessly.
• Discover new people and custom feeds.
• Send DMs and share your own posts.
• Pin new home screen widgets for your Following and Discover feeds.
